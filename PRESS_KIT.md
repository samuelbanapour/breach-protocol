## Long description
Breach Protocol is a free browser-based strategy game with two complete ways to play.

As **Blue Team**, you're an analyst in a Security Operations Center: waves of cyber-attacks —
malware, DDoS floods, phishing, SQL injection, ransomware, zero-day exploits, and Advanced
Persistent Threat bosses — race down the network toward your core. Each of your six defenses
(antivirus, firewall, email gateway, web firewall, EDR/backup, AI threat intel) is 3× against
one threat type, so reading each wave and mixing your layout is the whole game.

As **Red Hat**, you flip sides and play the attacker. Spend intel to launch the attacks the
enemy's defenses can't counter, breach their core, and keep your Trace meter under 100% before
the (smart, self-escalating) blue team locates you — using tools like DDoS Surge and a Zero-Day
cloak.

It's quick to learn, hard to master, runs instantly in any browser, and ships with 65
achievements and saved progress.

## Key features
- Two complete modes: defend (tower-defense) and attack (reverse tower-defense)
- 6 threat types + APT bosses, countered by 6 defenses with a strengths/weaknesses system
- 65 unlockable achievements with saved progress and best-run records
- Smart, self-escalating AI; upgrades, special abilities, and an in-game economy
- Instant play — no download; also available as downloadable desktop & Android builds
